Health Information for Gen Alpha

Learn about nutrition, exercise, and common health issues.

Nutrition

Eating a balanced diet is key to maintaining health. Make sure to include fruits, vegetables, proteins, and whole grains in your meals. Avoid sugary drinks and processed snacks.

Exercise

Daily exercise helps keep the body strong and healthy. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity, like walking, biking, or playing sports.

Common Health Issues

Pay attention to common issues like dehydration, lack of sleep, and obesity. Staying hydrated, getting enough rest, and maintaining a healthy weight are important for your overall well-being.
